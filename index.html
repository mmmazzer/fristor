ABOUT THE COMPANY

Fristor Technologies Sdn Bhd is a technology company founded in 2005 specialising in software development and building automation. For many years, the company has been providing customised solutions for building owners by integrating all the different systems and devices in a building onto a single open platform so they can communicate and work together seamlessly. The automation solutions that we provide are unique to the requirement of business owners to ensure efficiency. In recent years, we have expanded our team and our expertise to provide customised BUSINESS APPLICATION SERVICE to improve efficiency and boost the productivity of organisations that are concerned about business efficiency and are willing to embrace technology to automate routine manual tasks.

OUR WORK

Linen Management

The Linen Management System (LMS) tracks the business process and quantities of linen exchanged between the hospital and the outsourced vendor for laundry. This system uses RFID technology to track the linen. A web application identifies the person performing the tasks and recording the amount of linen collected and delivered to ensure no loss of linen when handled. LMS has reduced the amount of lost linen and minimised the financial loss to replace them.

Newborn Management

The Newborn Management System (NBMS) tracks the baby's bassinet position and mother's location using Bluetooth and RFID technologies. An integrated web application enforces the business processes such as baby registration, the registration of the mother's room, the transfers by nurses between baby and mother, and others.
